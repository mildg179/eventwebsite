Drag brunch is a type of drag show, in which drag queens perform for an audience while the audience feasts on brunch foods and drinks. Although it is typically held at LGBTQ bars and nightclubs, restaurants have also become a popular site for drag brunches which is where Perry’s Restaurant comes into play.

Drag brunch is the most important meal of the day! After months of hibernation due to Covid-19 it is finally time to throw on your best brunch fit, gather your friends, and enjoy D.C.’s best brunch accompanied by an exciting performance by the fabulous queens.
